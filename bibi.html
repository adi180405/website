A beautiful Mughal monument built in memory of Dilras Banu Begum.

History & Construction

The mausoleum was constructed between 1668 and 1669. The total cost of construction was around Rs. 6,68,203. Marble was brought from Jaipur. Though inspired by the Taj Mahal, it has its own unique architectural beauty and historical importance.
